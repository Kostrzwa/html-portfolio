The best movies according to Basia

My top 3 movies, according to my very subjective taste

Kotaro lives alone

A touching story about a boy who lives by himself due to some serious circumstances, and meets some people who show him that the world is not as bad as he thinks.

The Greatest Showman

If you love Hugh Jackmann, you have to see this movie. It is a romanticised version of the live of one of the founders of today's circus.

Nemona

A beautiful story about love and friendship, teaching us that no matter who you love, what matters the most is what kind of person you are.
